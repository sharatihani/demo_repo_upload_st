A Head Full of Dreams

a coldplay redesign

by Tihani

These four pieces were my final products of my A Level personal project.
I chose to redesign each of Coldplay's singles from their vibrant album, "A Head Full of Dreams".
Each piece of work has a visual reference to either the lyrics or the music video to the songs.
My inspiration for this project stemmed from Coldplay as an identity as well as research in to the Psychedelic era and visual graphics created in the 60s, as well as modern mixed media art.

elements & processes
